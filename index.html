About

About VibeSpace

VibeSpace is a desktop AI assistant designed to bring chat, voice, local models, plugins, and automation into one clean workspace. It is built for creators, developers, entrepreneurs, and power users who want one assistant that can help manage projects, code, research, reminders, and daily workflows.

Local-first by design — your chats, tasks, memories, and settings live on your device by default. Cloud features are opt-in, and you bring your own model keys.
